    <h1>Classic Pancakes</h1>

    <p>
      These classic pancakes are fluffy, easy to make, and perfect for breakfast or brunch.
      They can be served with syrup, fruits, or butter.
    </p>

    <h2>Ingredients</h2>
    <ul>
      <li>1 cup all-purpose flour</li>
      <li>1 tablespoon sugar</li>
      <li>1 teaspoon baking powder</li>
      <li>1 cup milk</li>
    </ul>

    <h2>Instructions</h2>
    <ol>
      <li>Mix the dry ingredients in a bowl.</li>
      <li>Add the milk and stir until smooth.</li>
      <li>Heat a pan over medium heat.</li>
      <li>Pour batter and cook until golden on both sides.</li>
    </ol>
